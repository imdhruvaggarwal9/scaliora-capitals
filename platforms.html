Private Equity

Growth and control investments in established businesses

Scaliora Capital undertakes growth and control-oriented investments in established businesses that demonstrate strong fundamentals, scalable operating models and professional management intent.

We partner with promoters and leadership teams to institutionalise operations, strengthen governance, optimise capital structure and accelerate sustainable expansion.

Our Private Equity Approach

  • Established businesses with clear growth visibility
  • Scalable revenue and operating models
  • Ethical promoters with long-term vision
  • Businesses ready for institutional governance
  • Strategic planning & capital structuring
  • Corporate governance & board frameworks
  • Institutional readiness & exit planning

Our Support Framework
